Introduction

Welcome, to the attractions page! This is where the site offers information and brillant images of these beautiful natural places that are a must visit when traveling to New Zealand. There's a total of three sections to read about on this page. The first, Fiordland National Park, which is a beatuiful valley surrounded by breathe taking mountains covered with a mix of bright green trees and pure white snow. The second, Tasman Glacier, a shiny icy trip to see a massive glacier that could take up a whole day to explore by boat. The third, Huka Falls, a powerful sight to see coupled with a stunning sensation when feeling the cool mist from the enternal waterfall. Oh Yeah! The anmial on the home page is a Kiwi Bird.
